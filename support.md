# CaffMeter Support

Need help with your caffeine tracking? We are here to assist.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I delete an entry?**
Swipe left on any row in the history list or long-press an entry to delete it.

**My Watch isn't syncing.**
Ensure both devices have Bluetooth enabled. Open the app on your Phone and Watch simultaneously to force a sync.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to iPhone Settings -> Apple ID -> Subscriptions to manage or cancel.
